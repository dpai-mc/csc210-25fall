Bus 1 Circuit Guide

Overview

This circuit sets Input B to 0000 0001 when bus 1 is high, otherwise passes TMP to Input B. It uses OR and AND gates to achieve this behavior.

Design & Logic

Bit0 output = TMP_bit0 OR bus1

Bits1-7 output = TMP_bits AND (NOT bus1)

Test to ensure output is 0000 0001 when bus1=1 and TMP when bus1=0.
