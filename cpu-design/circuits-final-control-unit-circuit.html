Final Integrated Control Unit Circuit

The following diagram shows the **Final Circuit for the Control Unit** and is the integration of all of the previous individual Control Unit Instructions. Some of the lines/connections are difficult to follow via these diagrams.

NOTE: This wiring diagram is **highly complex**. Lying out your circuit in a clean, consistent, and readable way is **critical** to ensuring that your final control unit will work as intended and greatly simplifies the debugging process.

Am working on updating to provide more readable circuit layout images.
