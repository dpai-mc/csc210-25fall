IO Clock Pins Configuration

On our CPU, we need to set outputs from our Control Section for IOCLK_S, IOCLK_E, IO and DA

These 4 pins are on the Control Section and just need 1-bit outputs at the CPU level so their states can be monitored.

The IO CLK_S output pin is down the right-hand side of our control section circuit diagram just below our FLAGS_S pin.

The IO CLK_E pin is down the left-hand side of our Control section circuit diagram just below the ACC_E and C pins.

Our IO and DA pins are down the right-hand side of our Control Section circuit diagram just below the Register sets and the END output pin.
