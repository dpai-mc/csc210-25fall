CSC210 - Week 12: Memory Hierarchy and I/O

Aloha, and welcome to **Week 12**. This week dives deep into the architecture of data storage and transfer. We will explore the tiered **memory hierarchy** (registers, cache, RAM, secondary storage), the importance of **big vs little endian** data ordering, the fundamental **LOAD and STORE** memory cycles, and the differences between **memory-mapped I/O** and **port-mapped I/O** for peripheral communication.
