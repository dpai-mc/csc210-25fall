Logisim Design Assignment

This assignment requires you to apply the concepts learned this week by building two fundamental circuits in Logisim 2.7.1 that will be essential for our final CPU simulation project.

Assignment Tasks

  1. Part 1: 8-Bit Ripple-Carry Adder
    Construct an 8-bit ripple-carry adder. Your main circuit should take two 8-bit inputs (A and B) and produce an 8-bit sum output (S) and a single final Carry-Out (Cout). You must first build a full-adder as a sub-circuit and then use eight instances of it to create your final 8-bit adder.
  2. Part 2: 3-to-8 Decoder
    Construct a 3-to-8 decoder. This circuit should have a 3-bit input and eight outputs. Based on the 3-bit binary input, only one of the eight output lines should be active (high).
  3. Part 3: Discussion Question
    In a text document or in your submission comments, explain how you would construct a 4-to-16 decoder using two of the 3-to-8 decoders you just built. What additional logic gate(s) would be required? Describe the connections.

Submission Requirements

  • A single Logisim project file (.circ) containing your 8-bit adder and 3-to-8 decoder in separate circuits within the file. Ensure all inputs and outputs are clearly labeled.
  • Your written answer to the discussion question.
